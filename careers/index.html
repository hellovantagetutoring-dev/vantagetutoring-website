What Vantage looks for.

This is a results-driven private tutoring role. We hire people who prepare seriously, communicate clearly and treat every session like it matters.

01

Academic excellence

Recent top QCE results, subject mastery and the credibility to mentor students aiming high.

02

Prepared, never winging it

You review content before sessions. Punctuality is absolute, online or in person.

03

Rapport that retains

You are the face of Vantage. Building trust and delivering value is how families stay with us.

04

Clear communication

Session notes logged promptly, parents kept in the loop, and teaching that actually clicks.

The opportunity, in plain terms.

Independent contractor role with competitive pay, flexible scheduling and students who are serious about improving.

How you’re engaged

  • Independent contractor (ABN required; free via abr.gov.au)
  • You invoice Vantage fortnightly with our template
  • Paid for contact hours; prep is built into the rate
  • Blue Card required (or application in progress)

Remuneration

  • Starting from $45–$50/hr online by year level
  • Up to $80/hr with experience and strong parent feedback
  • Opportunities to grow your rate as you build hours and reviews
  • Final rate confirmed after interview and contract

Successful applicants sign a contractor agreement covering confidentiality, non-solicitation and professional standards.
