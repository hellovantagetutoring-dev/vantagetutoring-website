Privacy Policy & Terms of Use.

How we handle your information and the terms that apply when you use Vantage Tutoring’s website and services in Queensland, Australia.

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This page sets out our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use for Vantage Tutoring (“we”, “us”, “our”), operating in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. By using vantagetutoring.com.au or engaging our services, you agree to these terms.

This document is a practical baseline for a Queensland tutoring business. It is not formal legal advice. If your business structure, refunds, or child-safety requirements change, update this page accordingly.

1. Privacy Policy

1.1 Who we are

Vantage Tutoring provides tutoring, assignment review, masterclasses and related educational support. For privacy questions, contact us at hello@vantagetutoring.com.au or 0414 131 893.

1.2 What personal information we collect

Depending on how you contact us, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number and preferred contact method
  • Whether you are a parent/guardian or student, school, year level and subjects
  • Availability, goals, tutor-tier preference and enquiry details you submit
  • Payment and booking details needed to deliver sessions (for example via invoice or scheduling tools)
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type and pages visited (via standard website hosting and analytics, if used)

We do not ask for more information than we reasonably need to respond to your enquiry or provide tutoring.

1.3 How we collect information

  • Directly from you (enquiry form, email, phone, SMS, Google Meet or in person)
  • From a parent/guardian on behalf of a student under 18
  • Through third-party tools we use to operate the business (for example Formspree for form delivery and Calendly for consultations)

1.4 Why we collect and use information

We use personal information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and match students with tutors
  • Schedule, deliver and improve tutoring and related services
  • Communicate about sessions, resources, payments and updates
  • Comply with Australian law and protect our legal rights
  • Operate and secure our website

We will not sell your personal information.

1.5 Children and students under 18

Our services are often arranged by parents or guardians for school-aged students. If you are under 18, please have a parent or guardian submit enquiries and enter into tutoring arrangements on your behalf where required. We treat student education information carefully and only share it with tutors and staff involved in delivering the service.

1.6 Disclosure to others

We may share personal information with:

  • Our tutors and team members who need it to deliver the service
  • Service providers who help us operate (for example form processors, email, scheduling, hosting or payment providers), under appropriate confidentiality arrangements
  • Regulators, courts or others where required or authorised by Australian law

Some providers may store or process data outside Australia. Where that occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure information is handled carefully.

1.7 Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

1.8 How long we keep information

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law (for example tax or record-keeping obligations), then delete or de-identify it where practical.

1.9 Access, correction and complaints

You may ask to access or correct your personal information, or make a privacy complaint, by emailing hello@vantagetutoring.com.au. We will respond within a reasonable time. If you are not satisfied, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

1.10 Cookies and website data

Our site may use cookies or similar technologies that are necessary for the site to function, or to understand basic usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

2.1 Agreement

These Terms apply to your use of our website and to tutoring and related services you purchase or arrange with us. If you book for a student under 18, you confirm you are a parent/guardian (or authorised carer) and can agree on their behalf.

2.2 Services

We offer private tutoring, assignment review, masterclasses/workshops and related support as described on this website. Session details, pricing and inclusions are set out on our Rates and Services pages, or confirmed in writing before you start.

We aim to match students carefully, but we do not guarantee a particular ATAR, subject score, university offer or other academic outcome. Results depend on many factors outside tutoring alone.

Nothing in these Terms excludes your rights under the Australian Consumer Law (ACL). If a service fails to meet a consumer guarantee, you may be entitled to a remedy.
