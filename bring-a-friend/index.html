How it works

01

Enrol together

Submit one enquiry with both (or all three) students, year levels, and subjects. Mention that you want shared sessions.

02

We match your tutor

Within 24 hours we pair you with a Gold or Platinum tutor who teaches your subject and can run a paired or trio session.

03

Learn on your shared rate

Sessions run on your usual schedule. Each student is billed at the shared hourly rate for their tier, year level, and online or in-person format.
